# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Mobile Coffee App - Product Details Page

## 1. Project Overview
The goal is to develop a high-fidelity, visually engaging "Product Details" page for a premium coffee application, based on the reference design provided. The page should emphasize aesthetics, smooth transitions, and a clear path to purchase.

## 2. Target Audience
- Coffee enthusiasts and connoisseurs.
- Users looking for a premium, convenient mobile ordering experience.

## 3. User Experience & Design Goals
- **Visual Style:** Modern, clean, and "premium" aesthetic. Use of high-quality imagery, sophisticated typography (serif for headings, sans-serif for body), and a warm, earthy color palette (browns, creams, muted greens).
- **Interactivity:** Smooth micro-interactions, especially during the scrolling and "Add to Cart" actions.
- **Clarity:** Ensure the product name, price, and primary Call to Action (CTA) are immediately visible.

## 4. Functional Requirements

### 4.1 Header Section
- **Back Button:** Standard navigation back to the previous screen.
- **Favorite/Wishlist Icon:** Toggleable icon (heart) to save the item.
- **Product Image:** A prominent, high-resolution hero image of the coffee drink.

### 4.2 Product Information
- **Category Tag:** Small label indicating the type of drink (e.g., "Classic", "Specialty").
- **Product Title:** Clear and bold name of the coffee (e.g., "Flat White").
- **Rating & Reviews:** A star rating display with the count of reviews.
- **Description:** A short, evocative description of the coffee's flavor profile and ingredients.

### 4.3 Customization Options
- **Size Selection:** Toggle buttons for Small, Medium, and Large.
- **Toppings/Add-ons (Optional):** A horizontal scroll or list of icons for additional options like "Extra Shot", "Oat Milk", etc.

### 4.4 Footer / Pricing Section
- **Price Display:** The total price, updated dynamically based on selected size/options.
- **"Buy Now" Button:** Primary CTA. Large, high-contrast button that stands out against the background.

## 5. Technical Considerations
- **Responsive Layout:** The design must adapt gracefully to different mobile screen sizes.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast for text and interactive elements. Support for screen readers.
- **Performance:** Optimize images for fast loading without sacrificing quality.

## 6. Success Metrics
- Completion rate for the "Buy Now" action.
- User engagement with customization options.
- Positive user feedback on the visual design and ease of use.
