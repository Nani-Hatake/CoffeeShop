The Preparation

Place the filter in the V60 dripper and rinse with hot water to remove any paper taste and pre-heat the vessel. Discard the water before adding 15g of freshly ground coffee.

lightbulb Pro Tip: Use filtered water for maximum clarity.
02

The Bloom

Pour 30g of water slowly, ensuring all grounds are saturated. Let it rest for 30-45 seconds. This allows CO2 to escape, preparing the coffee for even extraction.

waves Look for the degassing bubbles for freshness.
03

The Main Pour

Pour the remaining water in gentle spiral motions, avoiding the very edges of the filter. Keep the flow steady until you reach 240g total weight.
